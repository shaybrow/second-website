About Seamus

Long Story Short

My name is Seamus Brown and I'm a 25 year old guy (he/him) born and raised in Kirkland, WA. Most of my friends call me Shay but I don't mind either way. My family is originally from Canada but moved to the states before I was born. When I was 18 I moved to Bellingham and consider it my home town. I love the small town vibe and being surrounded by nature. During my spare time you can probably find me disk golfing, reading a book or spending time with my friends.
